Introduction to VR

Virtual Reality (VR) is an immersive technology that creates a simulated environment, often generated by computer technology. Users can interact with this environment in real-time, typically through specialized headsets or devices. VR aims to simulate the physical presence of the user within this artificial environment, allowing for a sense of immersion and presence that goes beyond traditional media.
